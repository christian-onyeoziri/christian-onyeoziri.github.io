Research Works

2020

Extraction of Bio-oils and Biodiesel from African Oil Bean Seeds

Christian I. Onyeoziri

Comprehensive research investigating African oil bean seeds as feedstock for bio-oil and biodiesel production. The study examined the effects of thermal pre-treatments on the quality and yield of bio-oil, contributing valuable insights to sustainable energy technology development and renewable fuel alternatives.

Renewable Energy Biofuels Thermal Processing Postgraduate Research
2014

Design and Construction of Mechanical Wheel-Powered Sprayer

Christian I. Onyeoziri

Engineering thesis presenting the design, construction, and performance evaluation of an innovative mechanical sprayer powered by wheel rotation. The research improved ease of operation, time utilization in spraying, and convenience across various land formations. Awarded Certificate of Merit for contribution to technology development at Scientific Equipment Development Institute.

Mechanical Design Agricultural Technology Product Innovation Award-Winning
2012

Construction of Tiger Nut Drying Machine

Christian I. Onyeoziri

Technical thesis documenting the design and construction of an automated tiger nut drying system. The research focused on reducing drying time while preserving nutritional content for use as a substitute for maize in poultry feed formulation. Included comprehensive nutrient analysis and performance evaluation.

Agricultural Engineering Food Processing Thermal Systems HND Project
2009

Production of Sandcrete Blocks for Poultry House Construction

Christian I. Onyeoziri

Practical research project on sandcrete block production for deep litter system poultry house construction. The study included strength analysis of produced blocks, quality control measures, and structural compliance evaluation for agricultural building applications.
