About Me

Passionate about engineering innovation and solving complex challenges

Engineering Innovation Through Precision

I am a results-driven Mechanical Engineer with over 7 years of comprehensive experience in product design, fabrication, and manufacturing systems optimization. My journey in engineering has been defined by a relentless pursuit of excellence and a passion for transforming complex challenges into elegant solutions.

With demonstrated expertise in 3D CAD modeling using SolidWorks and CNC programming with Fusion 360, I bring a unique blend of technical proficiency and hands-on production management. My approach combines systematic problem-solving with creative innovation, consistently delivering solutions that improve operational efficiency by up to 70%.

Throughout my career, I have led cross-functional engineering teams, managed multi-department operations, and pioneered innovative agricultural machinery designs. My work has been recognized with awards including the I-FAIR Israel/Nigeria Innovator Award and Certificate of Merit for mechanical engineering innovation.
